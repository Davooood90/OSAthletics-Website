About OSA Athletics

Old Scona Athletics Council

The Old Scona Athletics Council is a student led group that consists of student from across the school who all share a passion for athletics. They work with the Head of Athletics of Old Scona to organize athletic opportunities.

The council plays an essential role in ensuring that athletic opportunities are available at Old Scona every year. Through organizing events, we can promote sports and attract students to join school teams and stay active. We believe that it is important for students to have a vibrant life outside of their studies. Athletics offer students a space away from academics where they can hang out with friends, develop new skills, and most importantly, have fun!

We aim to encourage students to stay active by offering them athletic opportunities at Old Scona.
